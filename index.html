About me: I was born and raised in Sonoma County, at the heart of California wine country. I've traveled all over and have tried to live elsewhere but I keep coming back to my roots. There's no place better than the North Bay!
My passion: I LOVE herbs - planting them, growing them, and creating natural remedies with them. Plants are our allies and there's no shortage of knowledge to be gained by working with them. Plus they look pretty and make me happy!
Fun facts about me: I caught the festival bug one year, I followed Phish around the country and saw 17 shows in 5 different states. Once I bought a ticket to Ireland on a whim, left the same day, and left a note for my roommate that I'd be back in a couple of weeks.
